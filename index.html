Who We Are

SPIRAL Systems & Networks is a proprietorship firm founded and led by Mr. Love Chottani, operating from the heart of India's electronics hub — Nehru Place, New Delhi. As an Authorised Distributor of Portronics, we bridge the gap between one of India's most popular consumer electronics brands and retailers across the nation.

With our strategic location at 305, Red Rose Building, 49-50, Nehru Place, we serve as a one-stop distribution hub for the complete range of Portronics products — from wireless speakers and TWS earbuds to power banks, charging cables, USB hubs, and more.

Our Mission

To deliver genuine Portronics products to every retailer with competitive pricing, prompt service, and unwavering commitment to quality.

Our Vision

To be the most trusted and efficient distribution partner in the Indian consumer electronics ecosystem, empowering retailers with access to quality technology.

Our Values

Integrity, reliability, and customer-first approach define every transaction and relationship we build.
